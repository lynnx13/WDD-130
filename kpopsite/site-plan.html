Content

Home page

Why people come to my site
People will come to my website for a condensed information database on many different groups in the k-pop genre. I will also include information regarding how k-pop got started, so that people with all levels of knowledge feel welcomed.
What questions my visitors expect to be answered
Visitors to my site will be able to find the answer to any kind of questions related to popular kpop groups or kpop in general. For example, I plan on featuring information on popular groups, including their discography, members, and information about their agency and debut. I also plan to have an area of information on basic genre facts such as terms commonly used and big agencies.

What type of activities can visitors complete?
In terms of activities, people come mainly for information as it is a database of sorts. However, I will include a "if you like this group you might also like_" feature. To help people learn about other groups and to relate kpop back to english and americanized songs.

What information is necessary?
For one, basic information and understanding towards kpop is required, I would need to know enough information about it to teach others. However, as a visitor there is no level of knowledge needed. As I mentioned before, the site is made with beginners in mind.

Why will visitors come to my site?
Visitors will come to this website instead of others as it has a multitiude of content. Not only does it have information on popular groups but it also will explain commonly used terms and how kpop began. It removes the need for someone to go to multiple websites just to understand the one discussing different groups.

Introduction to page 2
Page 2 of my website will be dedicated to different popular kpop groups, both girl groups and boy groups. It will consist of links to smaller pages that have information about several groups.
Some groups that will be included are: BTS, blackpink, everglow, stray kids, ATEEZ, twice, red velvet, G-idle, itzy, aespa, txt, and new jeans.
These pages wil also include photos of the groups and each member, as well as the 'if you listen to this artist then you should listen to _' feature.

A snippet of what information about a group will look like:

"BTS debuted on June 12, 2013 with the single No More Dream from their first single album “2 Cool 4 Skool“. Their second single was Bulletproof Pt. 2. They released the EP O!RUL8, 2? on September 11, 2013 with the song N.O being the title track. In 2014 they released their 2nd EP Skool Luv Affair, with the title tracks Boy In Luv and Just One Day. In August 2014, they released their first full album, titled Dark&Wild, with the song Danger as the lead single. War of Hormone, another track of the album was promoted in October 2014. On April 2015, BTS released the EP titled The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Part 1, with the song I Need U as the title track. The song Dope was also promoted starting from June 2015. The EP The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Part 2 was released in November 2015, with the song Run as the title track.

These two EPs were part of their compilation album released on May 2016, titled The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Young Forever. This album had three MVs: One for the song Epilogue: Love Forever which was a prerelease track, one for Fire on May 2 which served as the title track, and one for Save Me on May 15. BTS’ second album was Wings, it was released on October 10, 2016 with the song Blood, Sweat and Tears as the title track. In February 2017, the released You Never Walk Alone, a repackage of Wings, with two MVs: one for Spring Day, released on February 13 2017, and one for Not Today, released one week later on February 20, 2017. Their 5th EP was Love Yourself: Her, it was released on September 18, 2017 with DNA as the title track. MIC Drop was another song that was promoted, and it’s remix from Steve Aoki had a MV on November 24, 2017.

Love Yourself: Tear was the third Korean album of BTS, released on May 18, 2018 with the song Fake Love as the title track. Love Yourself: Answer is another compilation album, released on August 24, 2018 with the song IDOL as the title track."
